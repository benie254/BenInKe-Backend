Benie Writes


Benie Writes is a blog application.

Admin users can:

  • Create an account.
  • Add, edit, or delete stories.
  • Add,edit, or delete poems.
  • Add, edit, or delete comments.
  • Add, or delete likes
  • Add, edit, or delete notifications.
  • View or remove subscribers.
  • View or remove contacts/messages.

Standard users can:

  • View published stories.
  • View posted poems.
  • View notifications.
  • Like stories/poems.
  • Comment stories/poems.
  • View story likes & comments
  • Reply to story/poem comments.
  • Like story/poem comments
  • Subscribe or unsuscribe to/from monthly newsletter.
  • Send email messages to the admin.
